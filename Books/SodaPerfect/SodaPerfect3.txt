-1970 Soda Perfect develops the Soda Sarsparilla, to market the North Columbian love for root beer.
-1974 Soda Orange gets introduced in Europe and Lechwia, as a popular beach summer drink.
-1984 Soda Perfect buys out Burger Lord.
-1990 Soda Blueberry enters the market, Lechwia. However, it doesn't sell well.
-1995 Caleb Junor steps down and leaves the company to his youngest son Roy W. Alterton as new brands other than Soda Perfect gain in popularity.
-2009 Soda Perfect fails to buy out the popular 2Can Brand.
In order to hold the water to the fruity 2Can caffeine drink, Soda Perfect develops and sells the new Soda LimyKiwi to new critical acclaim in 2010. Very fruity and very strong.
-2015 Soda Perfect buys the east peninsula off Coconutbay to create SODALAND. A tourist attraction to cover the companies expansion into military research.
