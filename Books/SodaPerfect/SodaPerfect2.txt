Chapter 3: Franchise Wars!

After old Calebs death, several copy cat products (Vardosian Wine, Good Liquid) tried to cash in on the poplarity of the drink. Given that the soda was sold in standard bottles, it was hard for soda perfect to be recognized easily! The company chose to create new custom and very unique bottle form which remained to be Soda Perfects staple bottle well into the 21st century.
As the second world war rolled in, sugar rationings were enforced upon companies by the government.
This was especially a looming threat on Soda Perfect, which had sugar as one of its major ingredientes.

1939 - Instead of altering its recipe, Caleb Junor made a special contract to solely produce soda perfect products to the Antillian army during the war. Other popular competitors like Candler's Cola or Brad's Popp were left out of that contract and did not survive the following sugar rationing, which Soda Perfect was exempt from.
